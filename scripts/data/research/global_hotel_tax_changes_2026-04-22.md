# Global Hotel Tax Changes — 2025-2026 Snapshot

**Researched:** 2026-04-22 · **Researcher:** Claude (WebSearch + WebFetch) · **Scope:** Recent accommodation tax changes, rules, exemptions across 30+ destinations

## TL;DR

- **31 jurisdictions** with concrete recent or 2026 changes.
- **4 major proposals** still pending (Thailand, Iceland, Prague, Balearics peak-season hike).
- Biggest dollar impact: **Amsterdam VAT 9% → 21%** on hotel rooms; **Belgium VAT 6% → 12%**; **Kyoto new tax up to ¥10,000/person/night**; **NZ IVL tripled to NZD 100**; **Bhutan SDF remains $100/night**.
- New taxes launching 2026: **Edinburgh** (5%, Jul 24), **Hokkaido**, **Kyoto**, multiple **Japanese prefectures**, **Wales** framework (2027).

## Europe

### Venice, Italy (IT-VE-VCE)
- **Day-tripper entry fee expanded 2026**: 60 peak days (was 54), €5 pre-booked / €10 last-minute. [source](https://www.timeout.com/news/venices-entry-fee-for-tourists-returns-this-week-heres-how-much-youll-pay-040126)
- **Overnight tax** (contributo di soggiorno) unchanged: €1.00-4.50/person/night, 5-night cap.
- *Confidence 0.85*

### Amsterdam, Netherlands (NL-NH-AMS) — BIG HIKE
- **VAT on hotel stays 9% → 21%** from Jan 1, 2026 (national change). [Gov source](https://business.gov.nl/amendments/vat-overnight-accommodation-goes-up/)
- **Toeristenbelasting** remains 12.5% of overnight price (excl. VAT).
- **Cruise day tax**: €15/passenger.
- Combined accommodation tax burden ≈33.5%.
- *Confidence 0.95*

### Barcelona, Spain (ES-CT-BCN) — DOUBLED
- **Catalonia IEET doubled** from April 1, 2026. 5* hotels: €7 regional + €5 Barcelona surcharge = **€12/person/night**.
- Barcelona municipal surcharge rises **€1/year through 2029** (will reach €8/night).
- Cruise passengers <12h ashore: €11/person.
- [source](https://ajuntament.barcelona.cat/turisme/en/latest-news/news/the-parliament-of-catalonia-passes-an-increase-of-the-tourist-tax-1607097)
- *Confidence 0.90*

### Rome, Italy (IT-RM-ROM)
- **Contributo di soggiorno** unchanged (since 2023): €4-€10/person/night by star, Airbnb flat €6, 10-night cap.
- **New Feb 1, 2026**: €2 Trevi Fountain access fee (non-residents). [Turismo Roma](https://www.turismoroma.it/en/news/tourist-tax-new-rates)
- Under-10 exempt. *Confidence 0.92*

### Paris, France (FR-IDF-PAR)
- **Taxe de séjour raised Jan 1, 2026**. 5-star business hotels: €9.20/adult/night, 4-star: €6.60, unclassified ceiling €15.93. [Service Public](https://www.service-public.gouv.fr/particuliers/actualites/A17929?lang=en)
- Since 2025: **Île-de-France Mobilités surcharge = 200% of base taxe** (transport + 2028 Olympics legacy).
- *Confidence 0.95*

### Berlin, Germany (DE-BE-BER)
- **Rate unchanged** at 7.5% net overnight price.
- 2026 administrative changes: quarterly (not monthly) filing, ELSTER e-filing required, small-business exemption <10 beds removed. [berlin.de](https://www.berlin.de/sen/finanzen/steuern/informationen-fuer-steuerzahler-/faq-steuern/artikel.57911.php)
- *Confidence 0.98*

### Greece (country-wide) — Climate Resilience Fee
- **Law 5162/2024 rates unchanged 2026**. Peak (Apr-Oct) per room per night: 1-2* €2, 3* €5, 4* €10, 5* €15. Off-peak ~30% of peak.
- Peak season shifted from March → April start. [source](https://news.gtp.gr/2025/01/07/climate-resilience-fee-rates-increase-for-greek-hotels-short-term-rentals/)
- *Per room, not per person.* Confidence 0.90

### Lisbon, Portugal (PT-11-LIS)
- **Taxa Turística €4/person/night**, age 13+, 7-night cap. Unchanged into 2026.
- Exemptions: medical-treatment travelers, students (first 60 days of academic year). *Confidence 0.85*

### Iceland (IS)
- **Reinstated 2024**, 2025 rates: ISK 800/unit/night for hotels/guesthouses (cat II-IV). Cruise ISK 1,000/port call. [Skatturinn](https://www.skatturinn.is/english/companies/tax-issues/lodging-tax/)
- **Proposal (Oct 2025)** for "considerably higher" tax — not yet enacted. *Confidence 0.95*

### Dubrovnik, Croatia (HR-DUBROVNIK)
- Peak (Apr-Sep): **€2.65/person/night**, off-season €1.85. Children 12-17 half, <12 exempt.
- City Council discussing further hike; nothing confirmed yet. *Confidence 0.85*

### Prague, Czech Republic (CZ-PRG)
- **CZK 50/person/night (~€2)** unchanged. Under-18 exempt.
- Proposals pending: CZK 100 national, CZK 200 for Prague 1 — neither approved. *Confidence 0.85*

### Vienna, Austria (AT-9-WIE) — PHASED HIKE
- **Ortstaxe 3.2% → 5%** on July 1, 2026. **Further jump to 8% planned for July 2027**. [Stadt Wien](https://www.wien.gv.at/english/e-government/financial/tax/local-tax.html)
- Base = net accommodation (excludes VAT, breakfast, 11% lump-sum deduction).
- *Confidence 0.90*

### Brussels, Belgium (BE-BRU) — TWO CHANGES
- **Regional hotel tax**: €5/overnight unit from 2026 (€4 for homestays/camping). [be.brussels](https://be.brussels/en/taxes-finances/tax-tourist-accommodation-establishments-higher-rates-1-january-2026)
- **Belgian VAT on hotels 6% → 12%** from March 1, 2026. [vatcalc](https://www.vatcalc.com/belgium/belgium-hotel-takeaway-meal-vat-rises-1-march-2026/)
- *Confidence 0.95*

### Edinburgh, Scotland, UK (GB-SCT-EDH) — NEW LEVY
- **First visitor levy in Scotland**. 5% on overnight accommodation from **July 24, 2026**, max 5 nights, charged before VAT. [edinburgh.gov.uk](https://www.edinburgh.gov.uk/visitorlevy)
- Advance bookings from Oct 1, 2025 trigger the levy if stay is from Jul 24, 2026.
- *Confidence 0.98*

### Wales (GB-WLS) — FRAMEWORK IN PLACE
- **Royal Assent Sept 2025**. Registration opens Autumn 2026. **Earliest levy implementation April 2027**.
- Rates: £0.75/person/night hostels/campsites, £1.30/night other. Under-18 exempt in shared accommodation.
- Each council opts in individually. Cardiff proposing adoption. [gov.wales](https://www.gov.wales/VisitorLevy)

### Switzerland (CH-GE, CH-ZH)
- **Geneva**: taxe de séjour CHF 3.75/person/night. **Zurich**: CHF 2.50/night.
- Cantonal rates vary CHF 1-7/night. No 2026 changes announced.

### Russia (RU) — MAX RATE DOUBLED
- Old resort fee replaced by federal **Tourist Tax** from Jan 1, 2025.
- **Max rate doubled** to **2% of room price** on Jan 1, 2026 (was 1%). Minimum 100 rubles/night.
- Now in 73 regions; Voronezh, Nizhny Novgorod, Izhevsk, Kursk, Samara added 2026.
- Paid by hotel operators (passed to guests via room prices).

### Balearic Islands, Spain (ES-IB)
- **ITS/Sustainable Tourism Tax** unchanged in 2026: €0.25-4/person/night + 10% VAT.
- **50% reduction from night 9** at same establishment. Under-16 exempt.
- 2026 peak-season hike proposal (to €2.50-6) **delayed to 2027** after industry pushback.

## Asia / Pacific

### Bali, Indonesia (ID-BA)
- **Tourist Levy IDR 150,000 (~$10)** per international visitor per entry, one-time. Unchanged in 2026. Gov. Reg. No. 6 of 2023. [lovebali.baliprov.go.id](https://lovebali.baliprov.go.id/faq)

### Japan (JP) — MAJOR WAVE OF NEW TAXES
- **Kyoto** (Mar 1, 2026): highest in Japan, tiered up to **¥10,000/person/night** for luxury. [timeout](https://www.timeout.com/asia/news/what-do-you-need-to-know-about-japans-new-lodging-tax-041026)
- **Hokkaido** (Apr 1, 2026): new prefecture-wide, **¥100-500/night** by price.
- **Hiroshima Prefecture** (Apr 2026): ¥200 on stays ≥¥6,000/night.
- **Gifu City**, **Toba City** (Mie): flat ¥200/night.
- **Nagano Prefecture + Matsumoto/Karuizawa/Achi/Hakuba** planned June 2026.

### Thailand (TH) — LONG-RUNNING PROPOSAL
- **300 THB entry fee** (air arrivals) — awaiting cabinet approval, Feb 2026 target but not finalized as of April. Land/sea fee shelved. [scandasia](https://scandasia.com/thailand-to-introduce-300-baht-tourist-entry-fee-in-2026/)
- *Listed in proposals, not findings.*

### Maldives (MV) — GREEN TAX DOUBLED + TGST
- **Green Tax 2025**: high-end establishments **$12/person/night** (was $6), guesthouses on inhabited islands with ≤50 rooms **$6/night** (was $3). [MIRA](https://www.mira.gov.mv/Pages/View/whatisgreentax)
- **TGST 16% → 17%** on tourism goods/services.
- Children under 2 exempt. *Confidence 0.95*

### Singapore (SG)
- **GST 9%** on hotel stays, SGD 1/guest/night tourism tax, optional 10% service charge. No 2026 change.

### South Korea (KR)
- **10% VAT** on hotels. Optional city tax $2-6/night. Hotel Tax Refund available for foreign tourists <6 months, <30-day stays.

### Bhutan (BT)
- **SDF $100/person/night** (international) through Aug 31, 2027. India INR 1,200; first 15k Bangladeshi/year $15.
- 6-12 half; under 6 exempt. [source](https://www.bhutantravel.travel/bhutan-travel-cost/)

### New Zealand (NZ) — TRIPLED
- **IVL raised NZD 35 → NZD 100** on Oct 1, 2024 (paid at NZeTA/visa). Review late 2026 for possible further hike.

## Americas

### Quintana Roo (Cancún, Riviera Maya, Tulum), Mexico (MX-ROO)
- **Visitax 285 MXN (~$16)** per international visitor age 4+, one-time. Unchanged 2026.
- Separate **state environmental tax** per room per night: Cancún 76 MXN, Playa del Carmen 54 MXN per couple.

### Hawaii, USA (US-HI) — GREEN FEE
- **TAT raised 10.25% → 11%** on Jan 1, 2026 under Act 96 (through Dec 31, 2030). [Hawaii DOT](https://files.hawaii.gov/tax/news/announce/ann26-01.pdf)
- NEW: **Cruise passengers taxed at 11% on prorated-in-Hawaii fare**.
- Extra revenue → environmental fund. *Confidence 0.98*

### Toronto, Canada (CA-ON-TOR) — FIFA TEMPORARY HIKE
- **MAT 6% → 8.5%** from June 1, 2025 through **July 31, 2026** to fund FIFA World Cup 2026. Reverts to 6% after. [Toronto.ca](https://www.toronto.ca/services-payments/property-taxes-utilities/municipal-accommodation-tax/)
- Bylaw 1259-2024. Expected to raise $56M. *Confidence 0.98*

### Montreal / Quebec, Canada (CA-QC-MTL)
- **Quebec Tax on Lodging 3.5%** unchanged. Stacks with federal GST 5% + Quebec QST 9.975%. Youth hostels exempt.

## Africa / Middle East

### Morocco (MA)
- **Taxe de Promotion Touristique** tiered: 15 MAD luxury, 11 MAD 5*, 8 MAD 4*, 5 MAD 3*, 4 MAD 2*, 2 MAD 1*, 1 MAD hostels. Under-12 exempt.

### Dubai, UAE (AE-DU)
- **Tourism Dirham unchanged**: AED 7-20/bedroom/night by star.
- Early 2026: 3-month **collection deferral** as hospitality relief (temporary, not policy). *Confidence 0.85*

### Saudi Arabia (SA)
- **15% VAT** on hotel rooms. Unchanged. International tourists can claim VAT refund.

### Kenya / Tanzania (KE / TZ)
- **Kenya**: 2% Tourism Levy on gross accommodation/F&B/services. Park fees $200 + 18% VAT (peak), $100 + 18% (low).
- **Tanzania**: 18% VAT. Plus $1-5 small local tax at some lodges.

### Turkey (TR)
- **2% Accommodation Tax** (Konaklama Vergisi). Unchanged 2026. Student dormitories, health facilities exempt.

## Observations

1. **2026 is the biggest year in recent memory** for hotel-tax changes globally. Overtourism and major-event funding (FIFA, Olympics) dominate the narrative.
2. **VAT bumps stack on top of tourist taxes**. Netherlands 9→21%, Belgium 6→12%, Maldives TGST 16→17% — these amplify already-rising tourist taxes.
3. **New-tax launches concentrated in 2026**: Edinburgh (first in Scotland), Kyoto, Hokkaido, multiple Japanese prefectures. Wales framework ready for 2027.
4. **Recurring exemption patterns**: age-based (<10/12/16/18 exempt), stay-length caps (5, 7, 10 nights), mid-stay discounts (Balearics 50% from night 9).
5. **Where the agent said "proposal" — often still is**: Thailand 300 THB entry (running since 2023), Iceland hike, Prague 4x hike. Don't seed these as live rates.
