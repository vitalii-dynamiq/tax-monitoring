## Patterns worth generalizing into the agent prompt

1. **Age exemption is universal** — almost every tourist-tax regime has one. Reasonable default prompt: "Consider whether age-based exemption exists under X=10..18."
2. **US 'permanent resident' is a specific legal concept** — stays ≥ N consecutive days automatically exempt. Research this for every US state.
3. **Jubilee / event-year surcharges have end dates** — important temporal modeling. Italy 2025 cases revert Jan 1, 2026.
4. **VAT stacks on top of tourism tax** — Netherlands 9→21%, Belgium 6→12%, Fiji 15→12.5%, India 12→5%. Must model both.
5. **Some countries exempt ONLY foreign residents** (BA) or ONLY non-residents (MY, where Malaysian nationals exempt from TTx). This flips the default.
