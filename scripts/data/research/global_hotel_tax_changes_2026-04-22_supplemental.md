**Researched:** 2026-04-22 · **Researcher:** Claude (WebSearch) · **Scope:** Destinations NOT in the primary `global_hotel_tax_changes_2026-04-22.md` file

## TL;DR

22 additional findings focused on 2024-2025 changes. Biggest ones:

- **Hong Kong** resumed 3% Hotel Accommodation Tax on **Jan 1, 2025** (was 0% for 17 years)
- **India** cut GST on budget hotels from 12% to **5%** on **Sept 22, 2025** — one of few places cutting, not raising
- **Melbourne/Victoria** introduced **7.5% Short Stay Levy** on **Jan 1, 2025**
- **Chicago** hit **17.4% total** — now highest in US
- **Norway** approved 3% framework for summer 2026; first cities Bergen/Tromsø/Oslo
- **Seychelles** new Tourism Environmental Sustainability Levy, Jan 1, 2026
- **Baja California Sur** "Embrace It" tax ($25) mandatory **Jul 1, 2025**
- **Italian Jubilee 2025** bumps in Milan (€7), Florence (€4.50-€8) — revert Jan 1, 2026

## Asia-Pacific — big moves

### Hong Kong (HK) ★★★ NEW TAX
- **3% Hotel Accommodation Tax RESUMED** Jan 1, 2025. Was 0% since 2008 (global financial crisis waiver).
- Applies to all hotel + guesthouse accommodation charges.
- [IRD official](https://www.ird.gov.hk/eng/tax/hat.htm) · [Legislative Council resolution Oct 23, 2024](https://www.news.gov.hk/eng/2024/07/20240703/20240703_150258_764.html)

### India (IN) ★★★ GST REFORM
- **GST on hotel rooms ≤₹7,500/night CUT from 12% to 5%** (Sept 22, 2025). No ITC.
- **Rooms >₹7,500/night remain at 18%** (with ITC). [source](https://taxguru.in/goods-and-service-tax/gst-rate-hotels-restaurants-effective-22nd-september-2025.html)
- Spa/health services: 5% without ITC.

### Melbourne / Victoria, Australia (AU-VIC) ★★ NEW LEVY
- **Short Stay Levy Act 2024 (Vic): 7.5%** on short-term (<27 consecutive days) accommodation bookings from **Jan 1, 2025**.
- Example: 4-night Melbourne stay at A$850 → +A$63.75. [source](https://harwoodandrews.com.au/news-ha/2025/3/31/the-short-stay-levy-act-2024-vic-implications-for-short-term-accommodations-in-victoria)

### Australian Capital Territory (AU-ACT)
- **5% STR levy** from **Jul 1, 2025** — same model as Victoria, lower rate.

### Malaysia — Melaka Heritage Tax
- Jan 1, 2025: properties <3-star **RM 2→RM 3/room/night**; 3-4 star RM 2→4; 5-star new RM 5.
- Country-wide TTx unchanged at RM 10/night (non-residents only).
- Selangor Sustainability Fee (proposed Nov 2025, effective Jan 1, 2026) pending.

### Vietnam (VN)
- **2% VAT reduction extended to 30 June 2025** — accommodation at 8% (not 10%).
- Hotels add 10% govt tax + ~5% service. No formal tourist entry tax enacted.

## Europe — Jubilee-year bumps and Nordic entrants

### Milan, Italy (IT-MI-MIL)
- **€7/person/night** from Jan 1, 2025 for certain accommodation types (Jubilee Year 2025 increase).
- Reverts to 2024 rates on **Jan 1, 2026**.

### Florence, Italy (IT-FI-FLR)
- **€4.50-€8/person/night** in 2025. Airbnb/B&B capped at €5.50/night from Aug 2025.
- 7-night max.

### Porto, Portugal (PT-13-POR)
- **€3/person/night from 2024** (was €2). Year-round, 7-night cap.
- Revenue up 42% in early 2025 per [Portugal Resident](https://www.portugalresident.com/porto-city-council-set-to-raise-tourist-tax-to-e3-per-night/).

### Funchal / Madeira (PT-30-FNC)
- **€2/person/night**, 7-night cap. Stable.
- NEW 2025: **€2/cruise passenger** disembarking at Funchal port.

### Algarve, Portugal (PT-ALG) — fragmented
- Various municipalities: **€2 peak, €1 off-peak**, 7-night cap.
- Only 7 of 16 Algarve towns collect as of 2025 per [idealista](https://www.idealista.pt/en/news/legal-advice-in-portugal/2025/08/05/70982-tourist-tax-in-portugal-2025-rates-for-lisbon-the-algarve-madeira).

### Norway (NO) ★★ NEW FRAMEWORK
- **3% tourist tax legislation approved 2025**. Opt-in by municipality. First adopters: **Bergen, Tromsø, possibly Oslo** — effective **summer 2026**.
- [AFAR](https://www.afar.com/magazine/norway-approves-new-tourist-tax-for-select-cities-regions)

### Budapest, Hungary (HU-BU-BUD)
- 4% of room rate + ~1000 HUF/person/night (~€2.48). Under-18 exempt. Stable.

### Romania (RO)
- 1% of accommodation cost per night. In tourist resorts, **only first night** charged. Under-18 exempt.

## Americas

### Chicago, USA (US-IL-CHI) ★★ HIGHEST IN US
- **17.4% total** after recent council vote. Stack: 6% state + 2% Illinois Sports Facilities Authority + 2.5% Metropolitan Pier & Exposition Authority + 1% municipal + other overlays. [source](https://travel-leisure.news-articles.net/content/2026/03/29/chicago-now-has-highest-hotel-tax-in-us-after-council-vote.html)

### Boston, USA (US-MA-BOS)
- 6.5% city + 2.75% convention center + 5.7% state ≈ **14.95% total**.

### Baja California Sur, Mexico (MX-BCS) ★★ NEW ENTRY TAX
- **"Embrace It" tax: 470 → 488 MXN (~$28 USD)** per international visitor age 12+, staying >24h.
- **Mandatory from Jul 1, 2025**. Prepaid online, QR code at arrival/departure. [Mexico News Daily](https://mexiconewsdaily.com/travel/baja-california-sur-bumps-uptourist-tax-fund-conservation/)

### Jamaica (JM)
- Tourism Enhancement Fee $20/international visitor. Landing $20 + Departure $35 (air). Stable.

### Bahamas (BS)
- **Cruise departure tax raised $18→$23 in 2024** + $5 environmental + $2 enhancement.

### Costa Rica (CR)
- 13% VAT on tourism services since Jul 1, 2023. $15 arrival tax. Stable in 2026.

### Aruba (AW)
- **$20 Sustainability Fee** per air arrival age 8+ from **Jul 2024** via ED card.

## Africa / Middle East

### Seychelles (SC) ★★ NEW SUSTAINABILITY LEVY
- Effective **Jan 1, 2026**: **SCR 75/night medium (25-50 rooms)**, **SCR 100/night large (>50 rooms)**. Small (<25) newly exempt.
- Residents, airline/yacht crew, children ≤12 exempt.
- [Tourism Seychelles official](https://tourism.gov.sc/?p=8950)

## Observations

1. **Biggest two-year window for new launches** in recent memory — Hong Kong, Melbourne, ACT, Baja California Sur, Aruba, Porto, Bahamas cruise, Norway framework, Edinburgh (first in Scotland), multiple Japanese prefectures.
2. **India is the outlier** — cutting, not raising (12% → 5% GST on budget hotels) to attract tourism.
3. **Italian Jubilee-year bumps** (Milan €7, Florence €4.50-€8, Rome already tracked) are **one-year only** and revert Jan 1, 2026. Important for temporal modeling.
4. **Caribbean consolidating toward flat sustainability fees** vs. percentage room taxes (Aruba $20, Bahamas $30 stack, Seychelles tier, Baja Sur $28).
5. **Chicago now holds the US crown at 17.4%**, overtaking NYC's ~14.75% and LA/SF's 14%.
6. **Common 2024-2025 exemption patterns**: <12/<18 exemption, 7-night caps, state-authorized local-option levies (Norway, Wales, Massachusetts).
